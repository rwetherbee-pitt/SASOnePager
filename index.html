Strength Across Schools

Integrating Computational Thinking, Computer Science and ELA

Dr. Cassie Quigley, Dr. Amanda Godley, Dr. Tinukwa Boulder, University of Pittsburgh

What is Strength Across Schools?

A medium RPP (Research-to-Practice Partnership) between a team from the University of Pittsburgh, school districts and regional educational organizations of Pittsburgh. This project focuses on integrating computational thinking and computer science principles into ELA classrooms

What is the goal?

Support underrepresented students in building computational identity, CS knowledge, and digital empowerment within an ELA context.

Create a regional Professional Learning Network of partner districts and teachers to provide ongoing professional development (PD)

Develop classroom-based teacher supports for implementing CT/CS curriculum in ELA classrooms.
